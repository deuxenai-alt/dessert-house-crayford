Our story

The dessert house

A small Crayford kitchen with one rule — never serve a dessert you wouldn't finish yourself.

We started with one waffle iron, one batter recipe, and the conviction that a 10pm dessert deserves the same care as a sit-down pudding. Every waffle is cooked to order, every cookie dough baked in front of you, every milkshake spun by hand.

The menu is long because the cravings are specific. Want a warm Lotus Biscoff cookie dough with vanilla gelato on a Tuesday night? You can have it. Want a Ferrero Rocher waffle with extra chocolate sauce? Take a seat. We're open until 11.

We're a small independent — order in store, click & collect, or delivery within a short ride of Crayford.
